About Us

India is a land of suffering, and its 35 million stray animals are faring even worse. We’re fighting to help the animals no one else will.More than 35 million stray dogs roam the streets of India, without food, veterinary care or refuge from its often punishing elements;We’re fighting to give them a chance.Because the behavior of most of the domestic animals is mainly observed by how we treat them,If we treat them with good care, love and compassion,they will definitely show the same love and respect towards us.
Every animal in the world needs to be taken care by providing them food, shelter and veterinary care.
